Pet Courier U.K.

If you are looking for a safe, comfortable and reliable service to transport your pet anywhere in the country or Europe then you have come to the right place.

We are a small family run business, we are committed to caring for your pets while in our care throughout their journey with us.

We can collect and deliver to your doorstop.

We cover the entire U.K and we can also transport throughout Europe. We work as a team with a minimum of two people meaning that throughout the entire journey someone is always with your much loved animal.

While in our care your pet will be exercised on lead, fed and watered at intervals throughout the journey. We can also accommodate any special requests that may be needed.

We believe that all animals should be treated as family and when we collect your pets while in our care they will be treated with love and the care as if they were our own.

Our vehicle has air conditioning and heating to make sure your pets are comfortable throughout their journey with us. We have digital thermometers in the thermo regulated custom built crates to monitor the temperature accordingly, your pets will travel on a soft vet bed with a fixed fan facing each crate which can be used whenever needed.

We are a DEFRA and APHA licensed type 2 transporter and fully insured. Please get in touch with any questions you may have.
